Road tile labeller 🛣️

Edges: N=top-left, E=top-right, S=bottom-right, W=bottom-left. Click an edge dot to toggle OPEN (asphalt continues → connects to a neighbouring road) vs CLOSED (curb/sidewalk → faces grass). The inferred type updates live so you can sanity-check. Then pick each road's role. Thin = 1-wide pieces. Thick = 2-wide road built from lanes (each lane has its pavement on the outer edge — mark that edge closed) plus thick corners/junctions; the pavement edges you mark give the orientation. Untick road for non-roads. Autosaves; copy the JSON when done.
